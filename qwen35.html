Key Findings — Qwen3.5 on b9484

9B: MTP still solid. n=2 with --poll gives 48.2 t/s (+39%). Essentially identical to b9198 (48.0/+38.7%). b9484 didn't move the needle here — good news, no regression.
27B: n=3 is slightly better now. mtp_n3_t16_pu at 16.5 t/s beats n=2 (15.9) for the first time. vs b9198 best of 16.7/+45%, we're close. Interesting that n=3+poll+ub works here.
35B: --poll + -ub is BEST, not worst. mtp_n2_t16_pu at 66.3 t/s (+14%) — significantly different from Qwen3.6-35B where those flags hurt. b9198 best was 62.6/+10.8%. So b9484 actually improved the 35B with flags: +14% vs +11%.
Cross-model anomaly: Qwen3.6-35B hates --poll/-ub on b9484 (drops to 7-8 t/s). Qwen3.5-35B loves them (best config). The model architecture difference (UD vs A3B, or Q6 vs Q4) is the divider. Use --poll + -ub for Qwen3.5 models only.
